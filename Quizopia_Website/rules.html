Quiz Rules

  • You must complete the quiz within the given time limit. If the time expires, the quiz will be submitted automatically.
  • There are no negative marks for incorrect answers.
  • Once the quiz begins, you cannot exit until it is completed.
  • After answering all the questions, click the "Submit" button to finalize your responses.
  • Ensure a stable internet connection to avoid any interruptions.
  • Read each question carefully before selecting your answer.
  • Good luck, and give it your best shot!
